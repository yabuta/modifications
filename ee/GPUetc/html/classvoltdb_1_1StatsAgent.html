Detailed Description

StatsAgent serves as a central registrar for all sources of statistical runtime information in an EE. In the future this could perform further aggregation and processing on the collected statistics. Right now statistics are only collected on persistent tables but that could be extended to include stats about plan fragments and the temp tables connecting them.
